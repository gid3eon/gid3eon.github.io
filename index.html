More About Me

Brandon Christopher McCartney, professionally known as Lil B and as his alter ego the BasedGod, is an American rapper. Lil B has recorded both solo and with Bay Area group the Pack.

His solo work spans several genres, including hip hop, new age, jazz, indie rock and choral music.

Once upon a time I, "Little B", released a 676 Track Mixtape called "Free Music: The Complete MySpace Collection". I then followed this up with "855 Based Freestlye Mixtape" consisting of, you guessed it, 855 Tracks.
